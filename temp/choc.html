Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks or 226 grams) unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup (150 grams) granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup (150 grams) packed light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cups (280 grams) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups (about 340 grams) semisweet chocolate chips

Preheat the Oven:

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats.

Cream the Butter and Sugars:

  • In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy. This can be done with an electric mixer or by hand with a wooden spoon.

Add the Eggs and Vanilla:

  • In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Pecorino Romano cheese until well combined. Season with a generous amount of black pepper (about 1/2 to 1 teaspoon), and be mindful of the salt as the cheese is already salty.

Bake:

  • Bake in the preheated oven for 9-11 minutes or until the edges of the cookies are golden but the centers are still soft. The exact baking time may vary depending on your oven, so keep an eye on them.
  • Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.
