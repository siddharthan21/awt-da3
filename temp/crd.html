  • Curd Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice (preferably short-grain rice like parboiled or sona masuri)
  • cup plain yogurt (curd) 1/4 cup milk (optional, for a creamier texture) 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1/2 inch piece of ginger, finely grated 1/2 cup grated carrot
  • 1/4 cup pomegranate seeds (optional, for garnish) 2-3 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil (preferably sesame oil) 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (split black gram) 1 teaspoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • A few curry leaves Salt, to taste

Cook the Rice:

  • Cook the rice as per the package instructions until it's soft and slightly mushy. You can use a rice cooker, stovetop, or pressure cooker to cook the rice.

Prepare the Yogurt Mixture:

  • In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the plain yogurt, milk (if using), and salt until smooth.

Mix Rice and Yogurt:

  • Add the cooled cooked rice to the yogurt mixture and gently mix it together. Use a wooden spatula or your hands. Make sure the rice is well coated with the yogurt.

Combine Tempering with Curd Rice:

  • Pour the tempering mixture over the curd rice and mix well.
  • Add grated carrot and chopped coriander leaves to the curd rice. Mix again.
  • Garnish the curd rice with pomegranate seeds (if using) for a burst of color and flavor.
